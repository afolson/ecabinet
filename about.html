About Me

My name is Amanda. I'm not sure what else to say about that.

I currently work for Linode as a marketing...person? I think calling marketers "people" is being pretty generous to us. I'd sell you my soul for 4 easy payments of $19.95.

I also worked as a writer/editor for the Linode Library, which is a pretty awesome collection of documents and how-to articles for Linode customers and general Linux users.

In my "spare time" (because really, who actually has that?) I like to read and write. I'm currently writing a book that I hope to have published next year, but I'm not holding my breath. I also dabble in code and do some consulting work. Most people would tell you about their hobbies here -- I guess I'm trying to say that burning the candle at both ends is one of mine.
